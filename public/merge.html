How to Merge PDF Files?

Combine multiple PDF files into one easily using File2do. Just follow these steps:

1. Upload Files

Click or drag multiple PDF files to the upload box.

2. Reorder Pages

Drag and drop files to change their order before merging.

3. Download

Click "Merge PDFs" and get your combined file instantly.
