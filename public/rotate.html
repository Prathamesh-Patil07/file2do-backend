How to Rotate or Delete PDF Pages?

Rotating or deleting pages in your PDF is quick and intuitive with File2do. Follow these steps:

1. Upload PDF

Click the upload area and select the PDF file you want to edit.

2. Rotate or Delete

Use the rotate icon to turn pages or click “×” to delete any page.

3. Download

Click "Apply Changes & Download" to get your updated PDF.
